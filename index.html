⚡ 4HM Jank‑O‑Meter ⚡

Filter rarely‑played cards, score your deck’s Jank, and validate 4HM legality with the current rules.

🔎 Quick How‑To

  • Filters: Narrow cards by set, color, type, rarity, mana value, and/or jank threshold. (The threshold value represents the maximum number of times a card has appeared in all decks in all prior 4HM tournaments as of T22.)
  • Deck Scoring: Paste your deck to get a Jank Score. Click cards anywhere (table or lists) to open the details modal.

📚 Learn More & Community

Four Horsemen (4HM) is a unique Old School Magic format using only cards from Arabian Nights, Antiquities, Legends, The Dark, the five basic lands, and ABUR duals. It’s an unsolved format where forgotten cards and wild brews shine.

The Jank score rewards decks that run oddball, rarely‑seen cards and penalizes heavy reliance on common staples. Extra credit is given when a high percentage of the deck is made up of these unusual picks. In short: the rarer and denser the jank, the higher the score.

      About Jank‑O‑Meter

      This tool defines Jank as any legal 4HM card that has appeared in tournament decks fewer times than the chosen threshold. Adjust the slider to reveal the deepest jank of the format.
